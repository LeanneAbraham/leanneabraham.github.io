About Me

Interactive Maps and Empathy

Tools: d3.js, leaflet, mapbox studio, html, css

Collaborators: Ross Thorn and Alicia Iverson

This project was created out of a desire to apply traditional interactive cartographic research and design thinking to an intentional graphic designed to explore the connection between cartographic theory and practice.

Specifically, it sets out to answer the question of "how do we design for an empathic response from the map viewer?" Human Trafficking at Home is the culmination of a semester-long research project where an in-depth literature review and expert interviews were conducted in an attempt to answer this question.

View as a seperate page: https://rossthorn.github.io/human-trafficking-viz/
